# Phase 20 — Wait status line: formatters & live driver

*Realizes design Decision 13 (the render-side half: the pure formatters and the live driver). Depends on Phase 19 (the gray palette register and the leading-separator/erase interaction) and Phase 6 (the render base).*

Additive to `internal/render`; nothing existing changes shape. Build the pure, table-tested `waitLine(model string, elapsed time.Duration, color bool) string` (yielding `waiting for <model> (<elapsed>)`, gray-wrapped when `color`, no erase prefix, no trailing newline) and `formatElapsed(d) string` (compact h/m/s rollover: `5s`, `2m17s`, `1h2m3s`), and the impure `LiveWaiter` driver — a goroutine + `time.Ticker` + real `time.Now` that stays silent for a **2s pre-roll**, then repaints `\r\x1b[2K` + `waitLine(...)` every **80ms**, with an idempotent `Stop` that halts the painter and writes the `\r\x1b[2K` erase only if it painted. `LiveWaiter` is the one isolated impure seam (the documented exception to the no-`time.Now`-outside-the-root convention); it structurally satisfies the `repl.Waiter` interface that Phase 21 declares, with no import cycle. It can land green and unused, the pure formatters proven by table tests and the pre-roll/erase behavior by a direct (slow-ish) test of `LiveWaiter`.

**Done when:** R-6DZ8-F5IK (`waitLine`/`formatElapsed` pure and table-tested — the line text and color wrapping, the elapsed rollover) and R-6HMX-KGQN (pre-roll and erase: a turn that ends before the 2s pre-roll paints nothing and erases nothing; one that outlives it paints and, on `Stop`, erases with `\r\x1b[2K`) are covered by clearly-named tests and the suite is green.
